# Academic Style Guidelines

## Core Goals

- clarity over ornament
- precision over vagueness
- consistent terminology
- claims matched to evidence

## Common Improvements

- shorten long noun-heavy sentences
- remove repeated framing phrases
- replace informal connectors with cleaner academic transitions
- tighten captions to what the reader should notice

## Things to Avoid

- inflated claims without evidence
- unnecessary passive voice everywhere
- changing the scientific meaning while polishing style
- rewriting notation or references unless asked

## Useful Scopes

- abstract
- introduction
- conclusion
- captions and tables
- rebuttal letters or responses to reviewers
